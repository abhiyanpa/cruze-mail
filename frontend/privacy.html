Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use CRUZE MAIL, the core function of the service is to generate temporary email addresses and receive emails on your behalf. The data collected includes:

  • Email Content: The emails sent to your temporary address are received and temporarily stored in our database for you to read.
  • Authentication Data: If you choose to log in via Google, we store your user ID and associated email to allow you to save inboxes permanently across sessions.
  • Technical Data: We may log IP addresses and browser metadata solely for security, abuse-prevention (Rate Limiting), and service monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to operate, maintain, and secure the service. Email contents are only displayed to the user who holds the corresponding inbox token.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Privacy is our priority. Our retention policy is strictly automated:

  • Temporary Inboxes: All generated inboxes and their received emails automatically expire and are permanently deleted after 24 hours (or up to 48 hours if renewed).
  • Saved Inboxes: If you authenticate and explicitly "Save" an inbox, it is retained permanently linked to your account until you choose to delete it.
  • We do not keep backups of expired or deleted emails. Once deleted, they are gone forever.

4. Security

We implement robust, modern security measures, including AES-256-GCM encryption for inbox session tokens and secure Firestore Rules to prevent unauthorized access. However, no internet service is 100% secure. You use this service at your own discretion.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or email contents to third parties. We may only disclose data if legally required to do so by law enforcement.
